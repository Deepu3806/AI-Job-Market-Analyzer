# Exploratory Questions & Findings

## 1. Which job title appears most frequently?

The dataset contains multiple analyst-related roles such as:

* Data Analyst
* Junior Data Analyst
* Business Analyst
* Quality Data Analyst
* Financial Analyst

Observation:
Data Analyst-related roles dominate the dataset.

---

## 2. Which locations have the highest number of job openings?

Frequently appearing locations include:

* New York, NY
* California-based cities
* Chicago, IL
* Washington, DC

Observation:
Major tech and business cities show higher hiring demand for analyst roles.

---

## 3. Which industries hire the most analysts?

Common industries identified:

* Technology
* Health Care
* Finance
* Consulting
* Non-Profit Organizations

Observation:
Technology and Health Care industries show strong analyst demand.

---

## 4. What salary trends are observed?

The Salary Estimate column contains salary ranges such as:

* 37–66
* 60–90
* 80–120

Observations:

* Salary ranges vary significantly across companies.
* Higher-paying roles are generally associated with experienced positions and large organizations.

---

## 5. Which companies appear most frequently?

Several companies appear multiple times in the dataset, indicating recurring hiring activity.

Examples:

* Healthcare organizations
* Technology companies
* Consulting firms

Observation:
Large organizations tend to recruit analysts continuously.

---

## 6. What patterns exist in company ratings?

Company ratings generally range between:

* 3.0 to 4.5

Observation:
Most companies maintain moderate to good ratings.

---

## 7. What data quality issues were identified?

Problems Found:

* Missing values
* Duplicate records
* Inconsistent salary formatting
* Placeholder values such as "-1"
* Unnecessary columns

Observation:
The dataset required extensive cleaning before analysis.

# Data Cleaning Performed

The following preprocessing steps were completed using Python and Pandas:

* Removed duplicate rows
* Handled missing values
* Removed unnecessary columns
* Cleaned salary column formatting
* Standardized company names
* Processed location values

# Key Insights from EDA

1. Data Analyst is one of the most frequently occurring job roles.
