# Documenting EDA Insights

## Purpose of EDA

The purpose of Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) in this project is to understand patterns, trends, and relationships within the job market dataset before building dashboards and generating business insights.

EDA helped identify:

* Hiring trends
* Salary patterns
* Industry demand
* Data quality issues
* High-demand locations and roles

---

# Key EDA Insights

## 1. High Demand for Data Analyst Roles

The dataset shows that Data Analyst-related positions appear most frequently among job postings.

Insight:
Organizations across multiple industries are actively hiring data analysts, indicating strong market demand for analytical roles.

---

## 2. Major Cities Dominate Hiring

Locations such as New York, California-based cities, Chicago, and Washington DC contain a large number of analyst job postings.

Insight:
Analyst opportunities are concentrated in major business and technology hubs.

---

## 3. Technology and Healthcare Industries Lead Recruitment

Technology and Healthcare sectors appear repeatedly throughout the dataset.

Insight:
These industries rely heavily on data-driven decision-making and therefore show higher demand for analyst roles.

---

## 4. Salary Ranges Vary Across Companies

Salary estimates differ significantly depending on:

* Company
* Location
* Industry
* Role seniority

Insight:
Experienced roles and large organizations tend to offer higher salaries.

---

## 5. Data Cleaning Was Essential

The raw dataset contained:

* Missing values
* Duplicate records
* Inconsistent salary formats
* Placeholder values

Insight:
Real-world datasets require preprocessing before meaningful analysis and visualization can be performed.

---

## 6. Company Ratings Mostly Remain Moderate to High

Most company ratings range between 3.0 and 4.5.

Insight:
Many organizations hiring analysts maintain relatively positive employee ratings.

---

# Business Understanding from EDA

The EDA process demonstrates that:

* Data analytics skills are highly relevant in today’s job market
* Job opportunities are strongest in urban and tech-focused regions
* Industry type strongly influences salary and hiring demand
* Clean and structured data is critical for business intelligence reporting

---

# Importance of EDA in This Project

EDA played a crucial role in:

* Understanding dataset structure
* Identifying data quality problems
* Preparing data for Power BI dashboards
* Discovering meaningful business insights
* Supporting data-driven storytelling
